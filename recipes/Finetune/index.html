Fine-tune a pre-trained modelÔÉÅ

After pre-training on public available datasets, the ASR model is already capable of performing general speech recognition with relatively high accuracy. However, the accuracy could be still low on certain domains that are quite different from the original training set. In this case, we can fine-tune the model with a small amount of additional labelled data to improve the performance on new domains.
